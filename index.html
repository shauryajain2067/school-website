Parth Aggarwal of Class V Sparkles in Hackathon 2020!















The harder you work for something, the greater you'll feel when you achieve it” Once again, Parth Aggarwal of V Phoenix has proved that where there is a will, there is a way. Parth participated in the Hackathon 2020 Competition organised by Avishkaar League- Asia's biggest robotics, innovation and creativity competition for the students of classes I to IX. Over 30000 students from all over the country participated in this competition. VGS is proud to announce that Parth bagged the 1st position in the event. He was awarded with a cash prize of Rs 5000 and a gold medal for the same! He made a game- “Fight versus Corona” which is about destroying corona virus and eating food & medicine- a very unique game indeed. Congratulations Parth, well done !!




VGS’ 68 teachers qualify MCE – Proud Moment!!












The pursuit of knowledge and learning is unending. It helps one to stay updated and prepared for challenges of the future. To keep up with the everchanging needs of 21st century learners’ teachers of Venkateshwar Global School enrolled for Microsoft Certified Educator (MCE). The course’s objective was to enable the teachers to meet the requirements of today’s learners. The program focused on skills like Collaboration, Communication, Real World Problem Solving etc. The Microsoft Certified Educator (MCE) certification validates that educators have the global educator technology literacy competencies needed to provide a rich, custom learning experience for students. The school congratulates all the teachers for the efforts and wishes them the very best for future endeavours!


Venkateshwarians Shine at the Annual Interschool Fest


Budding talents of VGS participated in the annual Interschool Fest ‘The Quintessence’ organized by Brain International School On November 21, 2020.The event offered a gamut of competitions ranging from artistic skills to technological prowess that provided an engaging platform to unleash the talent of students. Samridh Gupta (Preschool-Phoenix) secured Third position and Devanshi Kochhar (class1-Orion) secured First position. Heartiest Congratulations to the winners!
